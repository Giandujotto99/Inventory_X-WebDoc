Layer 2 - Complete Interaction Policy

The policy decides who can open a session, whether they can write, which operations they can perform, how close they must be, and what happens if they lose range or line of sight.

Create The Policy

Create an InventoryInteractionPolicyDefinition Data Asset named DA_Policy_WorldChest_Default in Definitions/Rules or in a dedicated Definitions/InteractionPolicies folder.

Access

FieldTutorial valueMeaning
bAllowReadOnlySessionstrueAllows opening the chest in read-only mode.
bAllowWriteSessionstrueAllows modifying the chest.
bAllowReadOnlyFallbackWhenWriteUnavailabletrueIf another player has write lock, you can enter read-only.
bAllowWriteLockUpgradetrueAllows upgrading from read-only to write-exclusive.
bAllowResumeFromSuspendedtrueAllows resuming after suspension.

Capabilities

For the tutorial, set:

  • GrantedReadCapabilities: ViewExternalInventory.
  • GrantedWriteCapabilities: View, Deposit, Withdraw, SplitAcrossEndpoints, MergeAcrossEndpoints, SwapAcrossEndpoints, MoveWithinExternalInventory, SplitWithinExternalInventory, MergeWithinExternalInventory, SwapWithinExternalInventory, RotateWithinExternalInventory.
  • GrantedSuspendedCapabilities: only ViewExternalInventory and ResumeSuspendedSession, if you want to allow UI during grace or suspended state.
Mental model

Session mode decides read-only or write. Capabilities decide which concrete actions are allowed inside that session.

Queries

For the first chest, leave requester and primary owner queries empty. When you want restrictions, use Gameplay Tag Query to require specific tags on the player or deny forbidden tags.

Assign The Policy To The Provider

Open BP_WorldChest, select ExternalProvider, and assign DA_Policy_WorldChest_Default to PolicyDefinition.

Verification

  • The chest can open read-only and write-exclusive sessions.
  • If one player takes write lock, a second player can enter read-only if fallback is enabled.
  • Moving beyond the allowed range suspends the session according to the policy.
